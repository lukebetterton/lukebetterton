Dog Grooming Services

Jan is the resident Dog Groomer here at the kennels and has been grooming professionally for some years. Thornwood Kennels provides excellent facilities that allow for any dog, big or small, to be pampered. Nails, ears and anal glands are all attended to if required.

In addition to clipping, Jan is also able to handstrip terriers if preferred.

Should a home visit be more suited to your needs that can be arranged as we also provide a mobile grooming service. 

We can also collect and return dogs for a grooming session at the Kennels.

For more information please call Jan directly on 07716034521.
