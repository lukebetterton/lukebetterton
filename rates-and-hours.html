Rates and Hours

Opening Hours

We are open 7 days a week throughout the whole year with the exception of Christmas Day and New Year's Day.

Summer:   8:00am - 5:30pm
Winter:      8:00am - 5:00pm

We close at 4:00pm on Sundays.

We welcome anyone wanting to look around at any time, however in order to give you the fullest tour we can, we suggest visiting between 10:00am - 12:00pm or 2:00pm - 3:30pm.

Boarding Rates

We pride ourselves on looking after all our residents as if they are our own. Each one receives exactly the same attention as the next no matter what size, breed, or temperament. We can also provide discounts for family groups.

Please contact us directly to discuss your dog's individual requirements.

Guests staying with us can also have a shower and shampoo with our resident dog groomer at a discounted rate of £14.00. Please enquire at time of booking to ensure availability.

Payments

Please note that all charges commence on the day your dog arrives. When collecting your dog following their stay, please be aware of the following:

  • If collecting prior to 10:00am there is NO charge for that day
  • If collecting before 12:00pm (midday) there is a half-day charge
  • If collecting after 12:00pm (midday) there is a full day's charge

All prices include food, bedding, heating, and at least 3 walks per day. Prices are reviewed annually on 1st June.
