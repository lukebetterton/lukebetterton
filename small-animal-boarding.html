Small animal care

At Thornwood Kennels, we're not just about dogs. Lots of pets need to be cared for during their owner's absence. We can provide facilities for all sorts of small household pets.

We can cater to a very wide variety of animals, from mice to birds to ferrets, and even the occasional lizard. All of this may vary according to individual requirements; feel free to contact us to discuss your pet's specific needs.
